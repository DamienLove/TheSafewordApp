Contact Pings & Gentle Alerts
----------------------------
- Contact cards now expose one-tap **Call**, **Message**, and **Ping** actions for quicker outreach.
- Pings broadcast a gentle alert to linked SafeWord peers (~60% volume) and display a check-in notification.
- Custom audio hooks: place `androidApp/src/main/res/raw/safeword_alert.(ogg|mp3)` for the siren loop and optional `safeword_ping.(ogg|mp3)` for the ping tone.

Signing Secrets Required
------------------------
- Android: `ANDROID_KEYSTORE_BASE64`, `ANDROID_KEYSTORE_PASSWORD`, `ANDROID_KEY_ALIAS`, `ANDROID_KEY_PASSWORD`
- iOS: `IOS_CERT_BASE64`, `IOS_CERT_PASSWORD`, `IOS_PROVISION_PROFILE_BASE64`, `IOS_PROVISIONING_PROFILE_SPECIFIER`, `IOS_CODE_SIGN_IDENTITY`, `IOS_TEAM_ID`, optional `IOS_BUNDLE_ID`
